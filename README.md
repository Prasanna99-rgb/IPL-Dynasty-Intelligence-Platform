# Project Overview

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is one of the world's most competitive cricket leagues. Teams are often evaluated based solely on trophies, but championships alone do not fully explain franchise success.

This project introduces a data-driven framework to evaluate IPL franchises using multiple dimensions of performance, consistency, pressure handling, venue dominance, and championship success.

The result is a custom **Dynasty Score™** that ranks franchises based on their overall historical performance and competitive strength.

---

# Business Problem

### Traditional Evaluation Problem

IPL franchises are usually judged by:

* Number of titles won
* Fan popularity
* Recent performance

This approach ignores several important business questions:

* Which franchise consistently performs well across seasons?
* Which team performs best under pressure?
* Which franchise dominates its home venue?
* Which team has the strongest championship DNA?
* Which IPL franchise can truly be called a dynasty?

Organizations, analysts, broadcasters, and fans often lack a unified performance framework to compare franchises objectively.

---

# Project Objective

Develop a comprehensive analytics platform that:

* Measures franchise strength beyond trophies
* Identifies championship-winning characteristics
* Evaluates performance under pressure
* Measures home venue dominance
* Creates a unified Dynasty Score™ ranking system

# Solution Approach

The project was built using a multi-stage analytics framework.

---

## Phase 1 — Data Engineering & Feature Creation

### Tasks

* Data Cleaning
* Missing Value Handling
* Team Name Standardization
* Match-Level Feature Creation
* Season-Level Aggregation

### Output

Clean analytical datasets for downstream KPI calculations.

---

## Phase 2 — Franchise Intelligence Engine

### Goal

Measure overall franchise performance.

### KPIs

* Total Matches
* Total Wins
* Win Percentage
* Playoff Appearances
* Franchise Strength Score

### Business Insight

Identifies franchises with long-term competitive consistency.

---

## Phase 3 — Championship DNA Analysis

### Goal

Understand characteristics of title-winning franchises.

### KPIs

* IPL Titles
* Finals Appearances
* Championship Conversion Rate
* DNA Score

### Business Insight

Measures a franchise's ability to convert opportunities into championships.

---

## Phase 4 — Pressure Performance Index

### Goal

Evaluate performance in high-pressure matches.

### KPIs

* Knockout Matches
* High-Stakes Wins
* Pressure Win Percentage
* Pressure Score

### Business Insight

Identifies franchises that perform when the stakes are highest.

---

## Phase 5 — Venue Dominance Engine

### Goal

Measure home-ground advantage.

### KPIs

* Home Matches
* Home Wins
* Home Win Percentage
* Venue Dominance Score

### Business Insight

Determines which franchises create the strongest competitive advantage at home venues.

---

## Phase 6 — Dynasty Score Model

### Goal

Create a unified franchise ranking model.

### Components

* Franchise Strength Score
* Championship DNA Score
* Pressure Score
* Venue Dominance Score
* Championship Bonus

### Final Output

Dynasty Score™

This score provides a holistic ranking of IPL franchises.
